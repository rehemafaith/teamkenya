Dennis Mutinda

Web Designer

Denis, a passionate18 year Kenyan, who's guided by the principle that It's through making that we learn the best. He has an interest for STEM in general with an inclination to Engineering, Electronics and Robotics. He has worked on several projects on the Arduino and raspberry platforms. He also has interacted and coded in C, C++, Java, Python, PHP, HTML, Javascript, CSS, Bootstrap, among others. In his four years in high school, he participated in the National Science Fair getting to the National Levels. He looks forward to helping better the world through his immense passion in Electronics and Robotics. He also has a soft-spot for Basketball, Skating, astronomy, chess and playing video games.

Elly Sativa

Web Designer

Elly Savatia is an alumnus of Upper hill School. As an Innovate Kenya Finalist, he worked on The Stair- Ramp ,a solution on universal mobility for wheelchair users. He was also among a selected group of students invited to join The Junior Academy of The New York Academy of Sciences on merit basis. He would love to be an inventor and explore the possibilities of technology in solving real world problems. His dream is to become “the Elon Musk of Africa “and he plans to pursue technology entrepreneurship.
