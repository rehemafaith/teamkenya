Elly Sativa

Web Designer

Elly Savatia is an alumnus of Upper hill School. As an Innovate Kenya Finalist, he worked on The Stair- Ramp ,a solution on universal mobility for wheelchair users. He was also among a selected group of students invited to join The Junior Academy of The New York Academy of Sciences on merit basis. He would love to be an inventor and explore the possibilities of technology in solving real world problems. His dream is to become “the Elon Musk of Africa “and he plans to pursue technology entrepreneurship.
